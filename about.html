Our Story

Repair First. Replace Only When Necessary.

Appliance Repair 123 started with a simple idea: Charlotte homeowners deserve a fast, honest appliance repair service that shows up on time, fixes the problem right, and stands behind the work. No runaround, no upsell, no unnecessary parts.

We've built our reputation on same-day appointments, transparent pricing, and a 90-day warranty that means what it says. When we leave your home, the appliance works — or we come back.

We service all eight major appliance types and have hands-on experience with premium brands most repair companies avoid. From a basic Whirlpool washer to a Sub-Zero refrigerator, we bring the same level of care to every job.

Our technicians are background-checked, licensed, and carry the most commonly needed parts on the truck so most repairs get done in a single visit.
