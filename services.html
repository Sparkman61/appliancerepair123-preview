Refrigerator Repair

Call to Schedule

When a refrigerator fails, time is working against you. We prioritize refrigerator calls and offer same-day appointments whenever possible to help protect your food and minimize loss. We service all configurations — French door, side-by-side, top-freezer, bottom-freezer, and built-in models.

We work on all major brands including Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, GE, KitchenAid, Frigidaire, Maytag, and high-end models from Sub-Zero and Thermador. Most repairs are completed in a single visit.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Not cooling or not cold enough
  • Freezer works, fridge section doesn't
  • Ice maker not producing ice
  • Water dispenser not working
  • Leaking water inside or underneath
  • Excessive frost buildup in freezer
  • Running constantly or cycling too fast
  • Loud humming, clicking, or rattling
  • Temperature fluctuating unexpectedly
  • Control panel not responding

Washer Repair

Call to Schedule

A broken washer means laundry piling up and a disrupted routine. We repair all types — top-load, front-load, high-efficiency, and compact models — from every major manufacturer. Our technicians carry the most commonly needed parts on the truck, so most repairs are handled in one visit.

We service Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, GE, Maytag, Kenmore, Bosch, Speed Queen, and more.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Won't start or power on
  • Not spinning or agitating
  • Won't drain after cycle
  • Leaking water onto the floor
  • Shaking or vibrating excessively
  • Loud grinding or banging noise
  • Lid or door won't lock
  • Fills then stops mid-cycle
  • Error codes on the display
  • Burning smell during operation

Dryer Repair

Call to Schedule

A dryer that won't heat wastes hours and runs up your energy bill. We repair both gas and electric dryers and carry common parts on the truck for same-day fixes. Whether it's a blown thermal fuse, a failed heating element, a worn drum belt, or a faulty control board — we find it and fix it.

We service all major brands including Whirlpool, Maytag, Samsung, LG, GE, Electrolux, Speed Queen, and more.

Common Issues We Fix

  • No heat or insufficient heat
  • Takes multiple cycles to dry
  • Drum won't turn or tumble
  • Won't start at all
  • Squealing or thumping noises
  • Shuts off before cycle ends
  • Gas dryer won't ignite
  • Door won't close or latch
  • Overheating or burning smell
  • Display or control issues

Dishwasher Repair

Call to Schedule

Dishwashers seem simple until they stop working — then it's hand-washing every dish. We repair all brands and styles, from built-in units to drawer dishwashers. Most issues — a failed pump, clogged drain, broken latch, or worn spray arm — are straightforward fixes we handle in one visit.

We service Bosch, KitchenAid, Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Miele, Maytag, Frigidaire, and more.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Not cleaning dishes thoroughly
  • Won't drain after the cycle
  • Leaking water onto the floor
  • Won't fill with water
  • Door latch broken or stuck
  • Loud noise during wash cycle
  • Dishes still wet after drying
  • Detergent dispenser not opening
  • Control panel unresponsive
  • Film or residue left on dishes

Stove, Range & Oven Repair

Call to Schedule

We repair both gas and electric stoves, ranges, and wall ovens. Cooking appliance problems are especially disruptive to daily life — we aim to get you back in the kitchen same day. Our technicians are experienced with standard residential models as well as high-end ranges from Viking and Thermador.

We service all freestanding, slide-in, and built-in configurations. Brands include GE, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Samsung, LG, Bosch, Viking, Thermador, Wolf, and more.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Oven won't heat or reach temperature
  • Uneven baking or hot spots
  • Gas burner won't ignite
  • Continuous clicking on gas range
  • Electric element not glowing
  • Broiler not working
  • Oven door won't close properly
  • Self-clean cycle stuck or won't start
  • Temperature running too hot or too cold
  • Control board or display failure

Cooktop Repair

Call to Schedule

Cooktop problems can range from a single dead burner to a complete unit failure. We repair gas, electric coil, smooth-top radiant, and induction cooktops. Whether it's a residential drop-in or a high-end integrated unit, we have the experience to diagnose and fix it correctly.

We service all major cooktop brands including GE, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Bosch, Samsung, LG, Wolf, Viking, Thermador, and Jenn-Air.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Burner won't light or spark
  • Constant clicking after burner is off
  • Gas burner burns at one level only
  • Electric element won't heat
  • Induction zone not detecting cookware
  • Cracked or chipped glass surface
  • Control knob broken or stuck
  • Igniter sparking continuously
  • One or more burners completely dead
  • Surface doesn't heat evenly

Microwave Repair

Call to Schedule

Microwaves are one of the most-used appliances in the kitchen and one of the most frustrating when they fail. We repair countertop, over-the-range, and built-in microwave models. Many microwave issues — a blown fuse, failed magnetron, broken door switch — are repairable and much cheaper than replacement.

We service GE, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Samsung, LG, Panasonic, Sharp, Frigidaire, and more.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Not heating food at all
  • Sparking or arcing inside
  • Turntable not rotating
  • Door won't close or latch
  • Runs but doesn't heat
  • Display blank or error code
  • Buttons or touchpad not responding
  • Makes loud humming or buzzing noise
  • Interior light not working
  • Shuts off before time is up

Ice Maker Repair

Call to Schedule

Whether it's a built-in refrigerator ice maker or a standalone undercounter unit, we diagnose and repair ice makers of all types. Ice maker problems are often caused by a faulty water inlet valve, a failed ice mold thermostat, or a clogged water line — all of which we handle efficiently.

We service ice makers from Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, GE, KitchenAid, Sub-Zero, Viking, Frigidaire, and all other major brands.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Not making ice at all
  • Producing small or hollow cubes
  • Ice maker leaking water
  • Making ice too slowly
  • Cubes clumping together
  • Overflows and won't shut off
  • Noisy or grinding during cycle
  • Ice has bad taste or odor
  • Arm stuck or not sensing level
  • Unit frozen or jammed
